Song List

Side note: Since most of the songs I chose for this list
don't have lyrics (Except Blockbuster), I'll give a few words
as to why I like each of the songs.

The Armor of God by Xtrullor

It is by far one of the greatest song drum & bass songs ever!
It always gets me pumped up when I'm feeling down.
And I think the most crazy part about the song
is that it's an orchestral drum & bass song.
A combination that I've never seen done by anyone else.
other than Xtrullor himself!

To the Top

ATLAS by BICEP

While my only compliant for the song is how off beat the drums sound,
I cannot help but vibe to it every time I hear it.The song give offs
fun and cheery feelings of Summer time! Also passion just because.

To the Top

Secret Place by CloZee

It's very CHILL song. The beautiful vocals, the Japanese instrumentals
and soft cozy feeling I get when listening to it. All that together is
what this so great!

To the Top

Kaleidoscope by Champagne Drip

This song puts me in a 1980s space atmopshere. The bass drops make me feel
like the bass is vibrating gently on my ears. The song overall gives an aura
of being high in sky without anything holding you back.

To the Top

2011 by ColBreakz

The song is absoulute headbanger. Its retro dubstep style is just amazing.
It's probably one of the only EDM songs that I would play at my funeral
and have zero problems with that.

To the Top

Dance of the Violins by F-777

Not much that I can say about this song. It is a bop!
and there's no true way for me to describe how epic is. So I recommend it to anyone who wants something fun to listen to.
